History


This is a group of three members: ROY,STEVE and MAXIME (Trio-Coders) studying at the Catholic Universty of Eastern Africa who sat down and so the need of people having access to their constitution and other countries constitution. This idea came into our mind after seeing most of the foreign students or people struggling in a particular counry simply because they aren't familiar with that country's constitution. So we so the need of providing for them this web site to enable them be able to read and be familiar with the country's rules and regulations.

Vision and Objectives


Our mission is to try and make sure that all people with any kind of disability are able to use this web site because most of the web developers do not put the interests of the needy when developing their web sites. Our objectives include; Collecting the constitutional documentation of different countris. Collecting more views from both the locals and internationals on how they would like the web site to look like. Trying to see all kind of disability most of the peole have and be able to meet them in our web site. Comparing our web site with others to see the kind features ommitted and try to include in ours.
